About Me

Growing up I played a bunch of sports including Soccer, Basketball, Volleyball, and Track & Field. I learned to code with Python and Javascript, and built some personal projects in both languages; eventually I learned enough about computer science and about security vulnerabilities to start practicing basic penetration testing techniques.

Today, I am Security+ certified and actively pursuing my first offensive security certification before trying for my OSCP once I've accumulated the necessary industry experience. I've become way more confident in my cybersecurity skills but I'm eager to properly start my cybersecurity career and really accelerate my development.

On a brief personal note, some of my interests and hobbies still include watching and playing sports (although much less effectively); financial markets and trading options (responsibly); listening to audiobooks and podcasts; the beach; and watching anime, movies, TV series, and documentaries.
